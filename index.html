About us

At Shark, we bring the taste of the ocean to your table with a wide variety of freshly prepared seafood dishes, crafted with passion and care. Located in the vibrant heart of Tenkasi, our restaurant is the perfect destination for seafood lovers who seek authentic flavors, high-quality ingredients, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere.
